🧠 Output hierarchy

The report is interpreted in layers:
1. OCR text2. Layout / sections3. Medical entities4. Biomarker meaning5. Safety filters6. Next steps
Example: glucose 260 → high blood sugar / diabetes risk; haemoglobin 8 → anemia risk; blood pressure 160/100 → hypertension concern.
Safety-first medicines: age, weight, allergies, pregnancy, chronic disease, and report context are checked before any suggestion.
Important: Handwritten prescriptions in India should not be auto-turned into a fake prescription. Extract and explain, then suggest doctor review.
